function redSamp = ep_reduced_results(runid,sampStyle,ispolar,isomega,args1,args2,varargin)

This function extract results of reduced dynamics at sampled forcing frequencies/amplitudes in continuation of equilibrium points

REDSAMP = EP_REDUCED_RESULTS(RUNID,SAMPSTYLE,ISPOLAR,ISOMEGA,ARGS1,ARGS2,VARARGIN)

  • runid: id of continuation run of equilibrium points
  • sampStyle: samping style of excitation frequencies
  • ispolar: coordinate representation of reduced dynamics
  • isomega: is the continuation parameter forcing frequency
  • args1: a cell arry of rho, namely {'rho1,'rho2',...,'rhom'} or a cell array of real parts of z, {'Rez1','Rez2',...,'Rezm'}
  • args2: a cell arry of th, namely {'th1,'th2',...,'thm'} or a cell array of imaginary parts of z, {'Imz1','Imz2',...,'Imzm'}
  • varargin: 'plot-off', turn off plotting figures
