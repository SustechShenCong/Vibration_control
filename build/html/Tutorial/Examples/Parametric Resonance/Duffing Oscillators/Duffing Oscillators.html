The linear and nonlinear stiffness of the coupling spring is varying in a periodic fashion. Furthermore the damping coefficient is negative, which leads to self excitation. The resulting exponentially growing amplitudes are stabilised by nonlinear damping. For details see Thurnher, Haller & Jain, 2023
